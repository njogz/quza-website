Compare, Grow and Track your Investments

Quza is an investment aggregator that enables you to achieve financial security through goal based investing.

Get it now

SET A GOAL

Every individual has life goals that they need to reach in the short term or long term. Consider planning for a holiday, school fees, retirement or even setting up an emergency fund.

SELECT A FUND

Find a suitable fund based on your financial goals. Compare the various options and choose the best fund for you.

INVEST

Start where you are. Make one-time, weekly or monthly deposits. Adjust them whenever you want and track your portfolio.

SPECIAL FEATURES

It is our top priority to make your investing activities much easier.

Choice

We give you real-time analytics and statistics that track diverse fund performances, helping you to achieve your goals and grow your wealth,

Simplicity

We use analytical tools that compile financial data and allow you to create investment plans and make transactions using a simple interface.

Security

You can be confident that the data that you share with us will be secure. We ensure data security with regulation and compliance.

Automation

We work with workflow engines that allow you to generate necessary documents and financial instruments on the fly.

Efficiency

We give you unprecedented power by giving you access to advanced investment services so that you can manage your own financial operations.

Group

You will be able to invest as a group and see transaction reports that are divided into categories, which will help your group make collective decisions on how much to invest in each category.
