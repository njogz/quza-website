Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I invest with Quza?

Quza gives you convenience as you invest. We use the principle of measuring your investment progress towards specific life goals. We remove the hassle of searching for an appropriate investment vehicle by giving you diverse options on one platform. We also make the whole process from sign up to making investment deposits/withdrawals seamless.

How does Quza protect my privacy and keep my data secure?

We know security and privacy are important to you – and they are important to us, too. We make it a priority to provide strong security and give you confidence that your data is safe and accessible when you need it. We’re constantly working to ensure strong security, protect your privacy, and make Quza even more effective and efficient for you. We also built easy-to-use privacy and security tools like 2-step verification so when it comes to the dat you share with us, you are safe.

What is a mutual fund and how much interest can I earn?

A mutual fund is a kind of investment that uses money pooled from a number of investors like yourself to invest in stocks, bonds or other types of investment. Quza has a wide range of mutual fund options that offer attractive rates of return on your investments. You can monitor how much your fund has earned or lost daily.

What are your fees and charges?

Quza is completely free. Quza has made the process of making a deposit/withdrawal into a mutual fund so simple that you do not have to contact your fund manager to alert them of your transactions. For that convenience we charge you zero fees. All we ask is that you recommend the service to your network using your special account code.

What payment modes can I use to transact?

Quza accepts Mpesa and Debit cards issued from all Kenyan banks.
